Website Designer

As a Website Designer, you will be responsible for creating visually appealing and user-friendly website layouts and designs.

  • Create wireframes, mockups, and prototypes
  • Collaborate with project managers and developers
  • Design website layouts, user interfaces, and navigation structures
  • Select and customize fonts, colors, graphics, and imagery
  • Conduct usability testing and gather feedback
  • Stay up to date with industry trends
  • Communicate effectively with team members
  • Knowledge of Adobe Photoshop, Illustrator and Sketch
  • Strong understanding of user experience (UX) principles and best practices
  • Solid knowledge of typography, color theory, and layout design principles
  • Familiarity with responsive design and mobile-first design approaches
  • Excellent attention to detail and the ability to work collaboratively in a team environment
  • Knowledge of HTML and CSS is a plus, but not required
  • Degree in Design, Fine Arts, or a related field is preferred
